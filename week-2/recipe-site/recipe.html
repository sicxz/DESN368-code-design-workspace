Easy Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

This is a super simple recipe that anyone can make! It's perfect for beginners and takes only 5 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly or jam (any flavor)
  • A butter knife

Instructions

  1. Take two slices of bread and place them on a clean plate
  2. Open the peanut butter jar and spread peanut butter on one slice
  3. Open the jelly jar and spread jelly on the other slice
  4. Put the two slices together with the spreads facing each other
  5. Cut the sandwich in half if you want (optional)

About This Recipe

This classic sandwich is loved by kids and adults!
Fun fact: The average American will eat about 2,984 PB&J sandwiches in their lifetime.
That's a lot of sandwiches!

Remember: You can use any type of bread you like - white, wheat, or even toasted bread!
